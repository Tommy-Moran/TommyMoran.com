Frequently Asked Questions

What should I do if I disagree with HEART's recommendation?

HEART is designed as a decision support tool and does not replace clinical judgment. If you disagree with the recommendation, please discuss with the Cardiology team directly via the hospital switchboard (03 6166 8308).

Can I use HEART for pediatric patients?

HEART includes indications for neonates, but comprehensive pediatric echo appropriateness criteria may be limited. For pediatric cases, consultation with the Pediatric Cardiology team is recommended.

What happens to the clinical information I enter?

Clinical information is stored securely without patient identifiers for audit and tool improvement purposes. Please never include patient names, DOB, URN, or other identifiers.
